# Cleric Rod Mod (Vanilla+)

**A balanced, high-quality Cleric weapon for Hytale!**

The **Cleric Rod** introduces a dedicated healing weapon that fits perfectly into the vanilla game. Designed for support players who want to heal their allies while dealing damage to the undead.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Primary Attack (Poison Bolt)**: Deals 15 Damage and applies Poison DoT.
- **Secondary Ability (Self-Heal)**: Instantly restores 20 HP with a right-click (1.1s Cooldown).
- **Charged Ability (AOE Regeneration)**: Hold left-click to channel a powerful **AOE** regeneration effect for **yourself and nearby players** (50 HP total).
- **Signature Ability (Area Purge)**: Press 'Q' to unleash a powerful area effect:
  - **Allies/Self**: Cleanses debuffs and provides **Healing**.
  - **Enemies**: Applies **Stun**, **Slow**, and **Continuous Damage**!
- **Custom Visuals**: Emerald-green particle effects, custom "Trinity" impact explosions, and a unique model.
- **Immersive Audio**: Custom sound effects for casting and impacts.
